Fragment Datasets

This table lists datasets containing peptide fragments for the predicted random coil regions of a proteome. If the required data does not appear here, you may generate a new dataset at the calculation page, selecting an appropriate UniProt proteome and parameters. Note that the calculation is placed into a queue may take several hours to complete, due to the high volume of random coil predictions required. FASTA format files containing peptide sequences are provided for download. These are available as amino acid or nucleotide sequences.
